## Packages (simple baseline)

### 1) Indie License
- For: individual developers / freelancers
- Scope: internal use + customer deliveries for a single end-customer project
- Limits: up to 2 devices / 2 operators at a time (adjustable in contract)
- Includes: 3 months of updates, community support
- Commercial terms: pricing and exact limits are defined in the signed agreement

### 2) Team License
- For: small teams / startups
- Scope: internal use + customer deliveries
- Limits: up to 10 devices / 10 operators (adjustable in contract)
- Includes: 12 months of updates, email support, priority bugfixes
- Commercial terms: pricing and exact limits are defined in the signed agreement

### 3) Enterprise / OEM License
- For: companies embedding HidBridge into hardware/software products
- Scope: redistribution / white-label / OEM embedding
- Limits: negotiated (per-device, per-site, or revenue-based)
- Includes: SLA options, roadmap alignment, security fixes, long-term support

## What A Commercial License Typically Grants

Depending on the package, the commercial license agreement may grant:
- the right to use the software internally
- the right to build, modify, and run it in production
- the right to distribute firmware/binaries (where applicable)
- the right to embed HidBridge in products/services (OEM)

## What Is Typically Not Allowed Without OEM Terms

Unless explicitly granted in a signed agreement:
- public redistribution of source code
- sublicensing to third parties
- publishing forks or derivatives
